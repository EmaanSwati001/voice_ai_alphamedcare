# DemoCare Clinic - Billing & Claims Policy

## Insurance Claims
The clinic submits insurance claims on behalf of patients after services are completed.

## Claim Status
A claim may have one of the following statuses:
- Pending
- Paid
- Denied
- Under Review

## Common Reasons for Claim Denial
- Prior Authorization Required
- Incorrect Patient Information
- Expired Insurance Coverage
- Duplicate Claim
- Non-Covered Service

## Prior Authorization
Some medical procedures require approval from the insurance provider before treatment. If approval is not obtained, the claim may be denied.

## Appealing a Denied Claim
Patients may request an appeal within 180 days of receiving the denial notice. The billing department will assist with the appeal process.

## Outstanding Balances
Patients are responsible for any remaining balance after insurance processing.

## Payment Plans
Flexible monthly payment plans are available for eligible patients.

## Billing Questions
For billing-related questions, patients may contact the billing department during normal business hours.
