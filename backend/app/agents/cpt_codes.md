# DemoCare Clinic - Common CPT Codes

## CPT 99213
Office or outpatient visit for an established patient with a low to moderate level of medical decision-making.

## CPT 99214
Office or outpatient visit for an established patient requiring moderate complexity evaluation and management.

## CPT 99203
Office or outpatient visit for a new patient requiring low to moderate complexity evaluation.

## CPT 93000
Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG) with interpretation and report.

## CPT 71046
Chest X-ray, two views.

## CPT 72148
MRI of the lumbar spine (lower back) without contrast. Commonly used to diagnose back pain, herniated discs, or spinal stenosis.

## CPT 70551
MRI of the brain without contrast.

## CPT 80053
Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP), a blood test used to evaluate kidney and liver function, blood sugar, and electrolyte balance.

## CPT 85025
Complete Blood Count (CBC) with automated differential.

## CPT 36415
Collection of blood by venipuncture.

## Note
These CPT code descriptions are provided for informational purposes only. Patients should consult their healthcare provider or billing department for procedure-specific questions.
