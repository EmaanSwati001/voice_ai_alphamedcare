# AlphaMed Clinic - General Billing Policies & FAQs

## Denied Claims Policy
If an insurance claim is denied, the patient is notified via their Explanation of Benefits (EOB). 
The most common reason for claim denials is "Prior Authorization Required", which means the insurance provider needed approval before the service was performed.
For denied claims due to prior authorization, our billing team must submit a retroactive authorization request or file an appeal within 180 days of the date of service.

## Billing Disputes & Appeals
Patients have the right to appeal any claim denial or billing charge. 
Appeals must be submitted in writing within 180 days of receiving the denial.
For help filing an appeal, patients can contact our Billing Support Team directly or request a transfer to a billing supervisor.

## Payment Plans & Financial Assistance
AlphaMed Clinic offers flexible payment plans for patients with outstanding balances.
Patients can pay their bills in monthly installments over a period of 3, 6, or 12 months with 0% interest.
To set up a payment plan, patients must speak with a billing representative.
We also offer a 15% discount for bills paid in full within 10 days of the invoice date.
